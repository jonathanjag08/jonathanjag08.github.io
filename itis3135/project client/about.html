About Me

Skate Inspiration

Some of my skate inspirations are people like Yuto Horgiome and Collin Provost, and Lizzie Armanto. I really like the Japan Nike SB team in general, due to their style of editing and bag of tricks.

Skate Philosophy

Honestly its better to attempt a trick immediately than think about the trick itself. Thinking about doing the trick leads to hesitation, and hesitation leads to being scared to actually do the trick itself.

Skate Goals

I wanna hit a treflip and then get it down consistenly. I want to be able to pop-shuv a three-stair. i want to be able to backside and frontside 180s, and I want to start to look less shaky when doing tricks.
