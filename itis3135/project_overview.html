ITIS 3135 Project Overview

Skate Portfolio: Raymundo Munoz Cuevas

Project Overview

This portfolio site is designed to showcase Raymundo's street skateboarding skils, media, story and contact information with my goal of attracting brand deals, collaborations, and potential sponsorships.

Client Information

  • Name: Raymundo Munoz Cuevas
  • Organization: Street Skater
  • Email: raymundo.skates@gmail.com

Site Map

Page Design

Home
  • Intro Page featuring a skate highlight video of Raymundo.
  • Audience: Brand managers, sponsors, skate fans.
  • Will contain a short bio about Raymundo.
Skate Portforlio
  • Details past events, video parts, and skate edits Raymundo has participated in..
  • Audience: Brands reviewing skae credibility and experience.
  • Will include a short-write up of his skating style and highlights from each project
Media
  • Clips and photos, categorized by location and type of trick
  • Audience: Anyone who wants to visually experience his skating.
  • Will include embedded Youtube links or Instagram Reels
About
  • Tells Raymundo's personal story, how he got into skating, what skating means, to him.
  • Audience: Brands that want to connect with skaters on a human level.
  • Includes a written bio, skate philipsophy, and goals in life.
Contact
  • Contact form for business inquiries
  • Audience: Brands looking to collaborate or sponsor
  • Includes a contact form, email, and link to his socials

Dynamic Functions

The site will include a contract form on the Home and Contract pages, as well as an interactive media gallery on the Media page. Additionally, a map of skate locations Raymundo has visited.
