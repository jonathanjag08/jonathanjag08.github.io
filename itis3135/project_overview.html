ITIS 3135 Project Overview

Skate Portfolio: Raymundo Munoz Cuevas

Project Overview

This portfolio site is designed to showcase Raymundo's street skateboarding skils, media, story and contact information with my goal of attracting brand deals, collaborations, and potential sponsorships.

Client Information

  • Name: Raymundo Munoz Cuevas
  • Organization: Street Skater
  • Email: raymundo.skates@gmail.com

Site Map

Page Design

Home
  • The purpose of this page is to be the home page for my client. It'll contain a summary of everything and links to the other aspects of the site.
  • The audience of these sites are recruiters trying to cast my client in a theatrical production.
  • Content of this page will contain simplified information regarding my client. At the bottom will be a form to contact my client.
Portforlio
  • This page will contain all the shows my client has performed in, and her role in that stageplay.
  • The audience of this site is someone interested in what my client has been in, alongside to see her roles.
  • Will contain information about the play, the role my client starred in, and an external link to more information about the play.
Media
  • This page will contain clips from her performances, alongside pictures in costume and performing.
  • The audience of the site would be those interested in pictures of and clips of her performance.
  • Will contain image files and video files of the plays. There will be an interactable photo gallery.
About
  • This page will contain more information about my client, her story and general information any recruiters would like to know about her.
  • The audience of this site would be those interested in learning more about my client.
  • This will contain paragraphs and pictures talking about my client's life.
Contact
  • This page will be a more detailed contact form, alongside will contain information about contacting my client.
  • The audience of this site are those interested in contacting my client to perform in one of their theatrical events.
  • This page will contain a contact us form alongside links to her socials and ways to inquire with her.

Dynamic Functions

I plan on adding forms that are fillable to contact my client, alongside containing interactable pictures and videos of my client performing on stage. The home page alongside the contact us page will contain the form, and the media page will contain the interactable pictures and videos.
