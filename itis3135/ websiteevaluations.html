Website Evaluations

Good Website

Lacoste Heritage

Lacoste Heritage is an online shopping site for men's and women's clothing. Additionally, they sell children's clothing. It's divided into 8 sub categories: New In, Clothing, Shoes, Bags and Leather Goods, Accessories, Sport, Underwear, and Sale

  • C: The website uses high contrast between text and background, ensuring readability. The bold white text against dark or high-quality images makes information stand out.
  • R: Lacoste maintains a consistent color scheme, typography, and branding across the site. The navigation menu, button styles, and font choices are the same throughout, making the browsing experience smooth and cohesive.
  • A: Everything on the site is perfectly aligned, from the grid layout of images to the text and call-to-action buttons
  • P: Navigation links are logically placed together, making it intuitive to move through the site. White space is used effectively to avoid clutter while still maintaining a premium and elegant feel.

Bad Website
